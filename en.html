What's Inside

Everything a fundamental forex trader needs

Built around one core principle: weights should reflect what moves price now, not what best describes the economy in the long run.

Fundamental Strength Score (0–100)
Quantitative index aggregating 21 macro indicators across 6 thematic tiers. Three interpretive bands: <53 Weak · 53–63 Neutral · >63 Strong. Auto-normalised for missing data.
Economic Heatmap
21-column matrix for all 8 currencies showing real values, colour-coded by strength and publication date per cell. Instantly identify the leading and lagging economies.
FX Performance 1M
Real 30-day return per currency vs a weighted G8 basket (ECB/Frankfurter data). The only 100% lag-free indicator in the model — captures institutional flows in near real time. Weight: 28% (highest individual weight in the model).
COT Positioning (CFTC)
Weekly net positions from the official CFTC Leveraged Funds report (Futures Only). Contrarian logic built in: extreme positions penalise the score instead of amplifying it. Price confirmation filter (v5.7): COT score is attenuated when extreme positioning is not confirmed by recent price action. Weight: 5%.
Carry Trade Analysis
Dedicated page with rate strip, ranked pair table, 18-month spread history chart, central bank meeting calendar and live Risk-On / Risk-Off regime indicator.
AI-Generated Narratives
Daily fundamental analysis per currency generated by Groq AI (llama-3.3-70b-versatile) using live macro data and latest news. Informational only — not trading signals.
Economic Calendar
Real publication data (actual, estimate, previous) for upcoming economic events. Times auto-converted to the user's local timezone. Updated 3× daily.
Real-Time News Feed
RSS feeds from FXStreet, ForexLive, Reuters, Investing.com and all 8 central banks. Auto-tagged by currency and estimated impact level. Updated every 8 hours.
Pair Recommendations
LONG/SHORT suggestions based on score differentials. Three confidence levels: High (>20pt), Medium (12–20pt), Low (<12pt). Data quality percentage shown per pair.

Methodology

6-tier scoring model — fully documented

The scoring hierarchy mirrors how the forex market actually prices currencies: carry > momentum > monetary cycle > structural fundamentals.

The dashboard is designed to be a transparent, auditable complement to internal analysis systems — not a black box. Every weight, every source and every calculation method is publicly documented.

  • Official data sources only: CFTC, ECB, Trading Economics, central bank websites
