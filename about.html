About

Global Investing FX Terminal

A professional-grade foreign exchange monitoring platform that consolidates real-time price feeds, institutional positioning reports, central bank policy data, US Treasury yield curve metrics, cross-asset flows, and AI-assisted market narrative — all in a single, continuously updated interface. No login required.

What it is

The FX Terminal is a single-page dashboard designed for serious FX market monitoring. It was built to address a specific gap: professional-quality FX context is fragmented across institutional terminals, broker platforms, and government data portals. This terminal aggregates the most signal-rich publicly available data into a coherent, fast, and always-accessible interface.

Coverage spans real-time price feeds across all G8 major pairs, CFTC institutional positioning updated weekly, US Treasury yield curve with key spread metrics, cross-asset correlation data, live central bank policy rates and meeting calendars, and an AI-generated market regime narrative updated three times daily.

AI in the terminal

AI is used for two specific outputs in the terminal, both clearly labeled:

Market Narrative
A concise market summary updated three times daily, synthesizing rate differentials, cross-asset conditions, COT positioning, and macro events into a readable context. Includes a regime label: RISK-ON, RISK-OFF, MIXED, or NEUTRAL.
Market Signals
Four to six prioritized market signals published alongside the narrative, each including an instrument, directional bias, rationale, and confidence level based on multi-factor alignment across rate differentials, positioning, and price context.

All other sections — prices, COT data, yield curve, heatmap, news — consume market data directly. AI does not generate, filter, or modify price data.

Currencies covered

USD · EUR · GBP · JPY · AUD · CAD · CHF · NZD — the eight G8 major currencies, accounting for the substantial majority of global daily FX turnover.
