Activation

1
Rent the EA on MQL5 Market
Purchase or rent the GI FX Terminal EA from your MQL5 account. After payment, the product appears under MQL5 → My Products.
2
Attach the EA to a chart in MetaTrader 5
Open any chart in MT5, drag the EA from the Navigator panel, and enable Allow WebRequest for raw.githubusercontent.com under Tools → Options → Expert Advisors. The terminal canvas will render on the chart.
3
Copy your activation key from the MT5 terminal
The EA displays your key in the top bar of the canvas — formatted XXXX-XXXX-XXXX. Click the key to copy it. The key is derived from your MT5 account number and broker server name.
4
Activate the web terminal
On this web terminal, click Activate in the top bar (or at the bottom of the right panel). Enter your key, MT5 account number, and broker server name. All panels unlock immediately and remain active for 365 days per device.
